Privacy Policy

  • There are no tracking cookies on the website and no visitor logs other than Neocities/GitHub defaults
  • There are no analytics at all in any of the apps installed/side-loaded from this website. The only way I hear about bugs and crashes from side-loaded apps is from direct user feedback.
  • Apps installed from Google Play might include crash reports only (no tracking and certainly nothing that could be linked to an individual)
  • 3rd party libraries are avoided where possible and vetted before use
