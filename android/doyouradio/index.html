DO!! YOU!! RADIO

Unofficial Android radio app for DO!! YOU!!! RADIO, made in a few hours one night long before they had a functioning official app. Completely superseded by Radio


Download .apk

To install you might need to tell your Android device to allow side-loading apps: 1: Open Settings 2: Tap Security & Privacy 3: Select Allow Apps From Unknown Sources

You might get a further scary popup saying Blocked by Play Protect, this is just because it's a fresh new app and it's not on the Play Store, it's safe to tap Install Anyway
