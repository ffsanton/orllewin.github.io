playdate sekvens

Sekvens is a 16 step sequencer/drum machine for Playdate.

Features:

  • Sequencer grid, each cell has 10 volume steps (0 to 10) and a special stop command (-1). When a cell is highlighted hold B and use up and down to change step volume. Hold down B and press A to toggle the value (if the cell isn't empty it'll clear it, if it is empty it'll set the volume to 10)
  • Track mute toggle. Take tracks out of the mix and drop them back in again.
  • Effects section with low and high band pass filters and two delays.
  • Intuitive controls. B changes values, A moves to the next section. Sections are the main sequencer grid, the central mute section, and effects.
  • Load your own samples; a Playdate .pdx file is just a directory, you can replace the .pda files in the Sample directory and update the references in sequencer.json. This is a little technical but there will be full instructions on how to do it here. You can have up to 20 tracks though the ui gets a little squashed with more than 16.

Coming soon in future versions:
